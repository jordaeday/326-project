Welcome to Destination: Vacation!

Destination: Vacation is a web application that helps users plan their next vacation. Users can input their desired destination, budget, and travel dates, and the application will provide them with a list of recommended activities, restaurants, and hotels. The application will also provide users with a map of their destination, as well as weather information and travel tips. Our goal is to make vacation planning easy and stress-free, so that users can focus on enjoying their trip.
